We put together this website for an assignment for an NJIT graduate course: IS 601 (Web Systems Development). This course is about learning a variety of things that have to do with current software technologies, developing applications with Python and MySQL, learning about the fundamental concepts that deal with the Internet and programming, and collaborating with source code.

For this assignment, we have created a website dedicated to the History of the Internet. This includes the following:

  • Invention of the Internet
  • The Browser Wars
  • The Internet Wars
  • Dot Com Bubble
  • The People

We have taken the time to learn all about the history of the internet and everything that has led up to what it is today. You can view each of our pages about different aspects of the history of the internet by going to the navigation bar at the top and using the dropdown arrow under "The Videos" or click on one of the bullet points above to view our explanations and summaries of these events! We hope you find them useful and easy to understand. Even though the Internet hasn't been around for that long (just a few decades!) a lot has happened since. Technology has advanced and evolved in many different ways since the beginning.
